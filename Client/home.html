What are some important features to consider when buying a mobile phone?

While Smartphones are the most popular mobile phones today, some still like the old-school charm of a Feature Phone . With physical buttons and limited features, these mobile phones are the first choice of people who want a no-fuss phone merely to stay in touch. The OG phone maker, Nokia continues to be a major player in feature phones even today. If you want to buy a smartphone, the first thing to decide is the operating system you may be comfortable with -- Android or iOS. Designed only for iPhone and other Apple devices, iOS is known for its speed and security. On the contrary, Android phones are more common since they run on an open-source OS and are widely available. Several brands such as Samsung, Oppo, and Xiaomi, among others, offer Android phones.
